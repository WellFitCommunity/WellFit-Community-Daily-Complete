# AHRQ R21/R33 Pilot Site & Academic Partner Prospects

**Last Updated:** January 18, 2026
**Purpose:** Identify eligible lead applicants (academic/non-profit) and diverse pilot sites for AHRQ R21/R33 application

---

# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The AHRQ R21/R33 application requires:
1. **Lead Applicant:** Academic institution or non-profit (for-profits cannot lead)
2. **R21 Pilot Sites (3):** Urban safety-net, FQHC, Rural Health Clinic
3. **R33 Trial Sites (10):** Expanded network across diverse settings

This document identifies **Tier 1 prospects** with existing research infrastructure, digital health experience, and alignment with our clinical AI/CDS focus.
