# RESOURCES

## Finding Prospects

### Texas Resources
- **Texas SORH:** [https://www.dshs.texas.gov/chs/cnws/sorh/](https://www.dshs.texas.gov/chs/cnws/sorh/)
- **TORCH (Texas Organization of Rural & Community Hospitals):** [https://www.torchnet.org/](https://www.torchnet.org/)
- **Texas Association of Community Health Centers:** [https://www.tachc.org/](https://www.tachc.org/)

### National Resources
- **HRSA Data Warehouse (find facilities):** [https://data.hrsa.gov/](https://data.hrsa.gov/)
- **Medicare CAH List:** [https://www.cms.gov/medicare/health-safety-standards/certification-compliance/hospitals](https://www.cms.gov/medicare/health-safety-standards)
- **NACHC FQHC Directory:** [https://www.nachc.org/](https://www.nachc.org/)
- **Rural Health Information Hub:** [https://www.ruralhealthinfo.org/](https://www.ruralhealthinfo.org/)

## Qualifying Questions

Before adding to pipeline, verify:
- [ ] Located in rural area (population ≤20,000)?
- [ ] Serves Medicare/Medicaid patients?
- [ ] Has chronic disease patient population?
- [ ] Open to technology solutions?
- [ ] Has capacity for 15% match?
- [ ] Leadership stability?
