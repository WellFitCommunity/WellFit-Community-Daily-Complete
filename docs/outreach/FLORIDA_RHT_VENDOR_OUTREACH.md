# Florida Rural Health Transformation (RHT) Vendor Outreach

## Target Program
- **Program:** Rural Health Transformation Program (RHTP)
- **Florida Allocation:** $209,000,000+ (FY2026)
- **Administering Agency:** Florida Agency for Health Care Administration (AHCA)
- **Status:** Awaiting legislative approval to expend funds; RFA coming soon

## Key Information

Florida received **$209 million for FY2026** — the single largest federal investment in rural health care in Florida history. This is year 1 of a 5-year program.

### Program Focus Areas

- Innovative care models
- Technology integration
- Workforce investment
- Sustainable healthcare access for rural Floridians

### Procurement Timeline

| Phase | Status |
|-------|--------|
| Federal award announced | ✓ Complete |
| Legislative approval to expend | Pending |
| Request for Application (RFA) release | Coming soon |
| Regional collaborative grant proposals | After RFA |
| Informational town halls | December 2025 (in-person + virtual) |

### Town Hall Participation

AHCA is holding informational town halls (in-person and virtual) in December. **Attend these to:**
- Understand program requirements
- Make connections with AHCA staff
- Identify regional collaborative partners
- Learn about RFA timeline and requirements

### Partnership Strategy

Florida's program emphasizes **regional collaboratives** submitting grant proposals. Consider:

1. **Partner with rural hospitals** — They will be submitting proposals and need technology vendors
2. **Partner with FQHCs** — Federally Qualified Health Centers in rural areas
3. **Partner with rural health networks** — Existing networks seeking technology upgrades
4. **Partner with home health agencies** — AHCA specifically mentioned home health participation
