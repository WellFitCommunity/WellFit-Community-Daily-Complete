### Products & Services

#### WellFit Community Platform

**Senior engagement and wellness monitoring for community health**

- Daily wellness check-ins with AI-powered health analysis
- Voice-enabled interfaces accessible to seniors
- Caregiver coordination with PIN-based family access
- Community program integration and event management
- Emergency alert dispatch and escalation
- Multi-language support

**Use Cases:** Senior centers, Area Agencies on Aging, community health organizations, faith-based health ministries, rural health clinics

---

#### Envision Atlus Platform

**Clinical care management engine for healthcare providers**

- Real-time care coordination across distributed networks
- AI clinical decision support (40+ specialized services)
- Prior authorization automation
- Medical coding assistance (ICD-10, CPT, HCPCS)
- Revenue cycle management integration
- Population health analytics

**Use Cases:** Rural hospitals, FQHCs, health systems, ACOs, home health agencies, skilled nursing facilities
