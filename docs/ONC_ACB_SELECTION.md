## What is an ONC-ACB?

An ONC-Authorized Certification Body (ONC-ACB) is an organization authorized by ONC to test and certify health IT products against the ONC Health IT Certification Criteria. Certification is required to:
- Qualify for CMS Promoting Interoperability incentive payments
- Be listed on the CHPL (Certified Health IT Product List)
- Market as "ONC Certified EHR Technology"
