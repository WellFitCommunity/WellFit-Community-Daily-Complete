# CATEGORY 2: DIRECT BILLING (RPM/CCM/RTM)

## 2.1 Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) - 2026 Rates

CMS finalized significant increases for RPM in the 2026 Physician Fee Schedule.

### New 2026 RPM Codes

| CPT Code | Description | 2026 Rate | Change |
|----------|-------------|-----------|--------|
| **99453** | Initial setup & education | $21.71 | +10% |
| **99454** | Device supply (16+ days) | $52.11 | +8% |
| **99445** | Device supply (2-15 days) | $52.11 | NEW |
| **99457** | First 20 min treatment mgmt | $51.90 | +8.4% |
| **99458** | Each additional 20 min | $52.00 | +8% |
| **99470** | First 10 min (new) | $26.05 | NEW |

### Monthly Revenue Per Patient

| Scenario | Codes | Monthly Revenue |
|----------|-------|-----------------|
| Basic RPM | 99454 + 99457 | $104.01 |
| Full RPM | 99454 + 99457 + 99458 | $156.01 |
| New partial month | 99445 + 99470 | $78.16 |

### Annual Revenue Potential

| Patient Volume | Monthly/Patient | Annual Revenue |
|----------------|-----------------|----------------|
| 100 patients | $104 | $124,800 |
| 500 patients | $104 | $624,000 |
| 1,000 patients | $104 | $1,248,000 |
| 2,500 patients | $104 | $3,120,000 |

## 2.4 Billing Revenue Model Options

### Option A: Direct Billing (Highest Margin)
Envision VirtualEdge Group becomes the billing entity.

**Requirements:**
- Medicare Part B enrollment
- Clinical Director (MD/DO oversight)
- Credentialed clinical staff
- Compliant documentation

**Margin:** 60-80% (after clinical labor costs)

### Option B: White-Label for Provider Partners (Easier Entry)
License platform to providers who bill under their NPI.

**Revenue Model:**
- SaaS fee: $5-15 PMPM for platform
- Setup fees: $5,000-$25,000
- Implementation: $10,000-$50,000

**Margin:** 40-60%

### Option C: Hybrid
Direct billing for owned patient panels + white-label licensing.
