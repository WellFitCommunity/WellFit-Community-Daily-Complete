## How RPM Works

1. **You connect your device** using one of the guides above
2. **Your device sends your health readings** (blood pressure, heart rate, steps, etc.) to WellFit automatically
3. **Your doctor sees your readings** on their dashboard — no office visit needed
4. **If something looks wrong**, your care team gets an alert and can reach out to you

## Need Help?

- Ask your **caregiver** to help with the initial setup — it only needs to be done once
- Call the WellFit support line if you get stuck
- Your doctor's office can also help during your next visit

## Browser Requirements (for Bluetooth devices)

Bluetooth pairing works in these browsers:
- **Google Chrome** (recommended) — desktop and Android
- **Microsoft Edge** — desktop and Android
- **Opera** — desktop

**Not supported:** Safari (iPhone/iPad/Mac), Firefox. For Apple devices, use the Fitbit/Withings/Garmin apps instead — they sync to WellFit through the cloud.
