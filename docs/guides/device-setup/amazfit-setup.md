# Amazfit — Setup Guide

Connect your Amazfit watch to WellFit so your doctor can see your heart rate, blood oxygen, and activity data.

## What Gets Shared

| Data | How Often |
|------|-----------|
| Heart rate | Daily |
| Blood oxygen (SpO2) | Daily |
| Steps, distance, calories | Daily |
| Sleep duration and stages | Each night |

## What You Need

- A **Zepp account** (Amazfit uses the Zepp app, formerly Mi Fit)
- Your Amazfit watch **paired and syncing** with the Zepp app
- A phone, tablet, or computer with internet

## Supported Devices

- **GTR** series (GTR 3, GTR 4, GTR Mini)
- **GTS** series (GTS 3, GTS 4, GTS 4 Mini)
- **T-Rex** series (T-Rex 2, T-Rex Ultra)
- **Bip** series (Bip 3, Bip 5)

## Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Set Up Your Amazfit Watch

1. Download the **Zepp** app on your phone
2. Create an account and pair your watch
3. Verify your daily data is appearing in the Zepp app

### Step 2: Connect Amazfit to WellFit

1. Log in to **WellFit**
2. Go to **My Devices** → **Connect a Wearable**
3. Tap **"Amazfit"**
4. Sign in with your **Zepp account**
5. Authorize WellFit to read your health data
6. Green checkmark = connected

### Step 3: Verify It Works

1. Go to **My Health** → **Vitals**
2. Your heart rate and activity data should appear within 1 hour

## For Caregivers

- Amazfit watches are **affordable** — great option for seniors on a budget
- Battery life is excellent (1-2 weeks per charge)
- Help with the one-time Zepp account sign-in (Step 2)
- The senior just wears the watch after setup — everything is automatic
