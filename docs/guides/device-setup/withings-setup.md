# Withings — Setup Guide

Connect your Withings watch, scale, or blood pressure monitor to WellFit so your doctor can see your health data.

## What Gets Shared

| Data | Devices |
|------|---------|
| Heart rate | ScanWatch, Steel HR |
| Blood pressure | BPM Connect, BPM Core |
| Blood oxygen (SpO2) | ScanWatch |
| Weight, BMI | Body+, Body Comp, Body Scan |
| Temperature | Thermo |
| Sleep duration and stages | ScanWatch, Steel HR, Sleep Analyzer |

## What You Need

- A **Withings account** (the one you use with the Withings Health Mate app)
- Your Withings device **synced to the Health Mate app** on your phone
- A phone, tablet, or computer with internet

## Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Make Sure Your Withings Device Is Working

1. Open the **Withings Health Mate** app on your phone
2. Check that your device shows as connected and synced
3. Take a reading to confirm data appears in the app

### Step 2: Connect Withings to WellFit

1. Log in to **WellFit**
2. Go to **My Devices** → **Connect a Wearable**
3. Tap **"Withings"**
4. You'll be taken to Withings' website to **sign in**
5. Withings will ask: "Allow WellFit to access your data?" — tap **"Allow"**
6. You'll be sent back to WellFit with a green checkmark

### Step 3: Verify It Works

1. Go to **My Health** → **Vitals**
2. You should see your latest readings
3. If the data isn't there yet, wait **1 hour** for the first sync

## How It Works After Setup

- Your Withings device syncs to the Health Mate app as usual
- WellFit receives **real-time notifications** when new readings arrive
- Blood pressure, weight, and temperature readings appear within minutes
- Your doctor sees trends over time on their dashboard

## Troubleshooting

### "I don't see my data in WellFit"
- Sync your device in the Health Mate app (open the app near your device)
- Wait up to 1 hour for the first sync
- Check **My Devices** for connection status

### "Connection expired"
- Tap **"Reconnect"** and sign in to Withings again

## For Caregivers

- The one-time setup (Step 2) is the only part that needs help
- After that, the senior just uses their Withings device normally
- Blood pressure cuffs are especially valuable — the readings go straight to the doctor
