# Fitbit — Setup Guide

Connect your Fitbit watch or tracker to WellFit so your doctor can see your heart rate, steps, sleep, and activity.

## What Gets Shared

| Data | How Often |
|------|-----------|
| Heart rate (resting) | Daily |
| Blood oxygen (SpO2) | Daily |
| Steps, distance, calories | Daily |
| Sleep duration and stages | Each night |
| Active minutes | Daily |

## What You Need

- A **Fitbit account** (the one you use with the Fitbit app)
- Your Fitbit device **synced to the Fitbit app** on your phone
- A phone, tablet, or computer with internet

**Note:** Your Fitbit talks to its phone app first, then WellFit reads from Fitbit's servers. You do NOT need Bluetooth on your computer — just your Fitbit synced to the Fitbit app as usual.

## Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Make Sure Your Fitbit Is Working

1. Open the **Fitbit app** on your phone
2. Check that your device shows as **connected** and synced
3. If not, follow Fitbit's instructions to set up your device first

### Step 2: Connect Fitbit to WellFit

1. Log in to **WellFit**
2. Go to **My Devices** → **Connect a Wearable**
3. Tap **"Fitbit"**
4. You'll be taken to Fitbit's website to **sign in**
5. Fitbit will ask: "Allow WellFit to access your data?" — tap **"Allow All"**
6. You'll be sent back to WellFit with a green checkmark

### Step 3: Verify It Works

1. Go to **My Health** → **Vitals**
2. You should see your latest heart rate, steps, and sleep data
3. If the data isn't there yet, wait **1 hour** — Fitbit syncs periodically

## How It Works After Setup

- Your Fitbit syncs to the Fitbit app as usual
- WellFit automatically pulls your data from Fitbit every few hours
- When new data arrives, WellFit can also receive **real-time notifications** from Fitbit
- Your doctor sees your readings on their dashboard
- If your heart rate or SpO2 is concerning, your care team gets an alert

## Troubleshooting

### "I don't see my Fitbit data in WellFit"
- Make sure your Fitbit is **synced** in the Fitbit app (pull down to refresh)
- Wait up to **1 hour** for the first sync
- Go to **My Devices** and check the connection status

### "It says my connection expired"
- Tap **"Reconnect"** on the Fitbit card in My Devices
- Sign in to Fitbit again — this refreshes the connection

### "I got a new Fitbit"
- Set up the new Fitbit in the Fitbit app first
- The WellFit connection stays the same — it connects to your **Fitbit account**, not the specific device

## For Caregivers

- Help the senior through the one-time Fitbit sign-in (Step 2)
- Make sure the Fitbit app is installed and syncing on their phone
- The senior doesn't need to do anything after setup — it's automatic
- Check the WellFit dashboard weekly to confirm data is flowing
