# iHealth — Setup Guide

Connect your iHealth devices to WellFit so your doctor can see your blood pressure, glucose, and weight readings.

## What Gets Shared

| Data | Devices |
|------|---------|
| Blood pressure | iHealth Clear, iHealth Ease, iHealth Feel |
| Blood glucose | iHealth Smart, iHealth Align |
| Weight | iHealth Lite, iHealth Core |
| Blood oxygen (SpO2) | iHealth Air |

## What You Need

- An **iHealth account** (from the iHealth MyVitals app)
- Your iHealth device **paired** with the iHealth app on your phone
- A phone, tablet, or computer with internet

**Why iHealth?** iHealth devices are **FDA-cleared** medical devices. They are designed for accuracy and are trusted by doctors.

## Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Set Up Your iHealth Device

1. Download the **iHealth MyVitals** app on your phone
2. Create an account and pair your device following the app instructions
3. Take a test reading to confirm it works

### Step 2: Connect iHealth to WellFit

1. Log in to **WellFit**
2. Go to **My Devices** → **Connect a Wearable**
3. Tap **"iHealth"**
4. Sign in with your **iHealth account**
5. Authorize WellFit to read your health data
6. You'll see a green checkmark when connected

### Step 3: Verify It Works

1. Take a reading with your iHealth device
2. Check that it appears in WellFit under **My Health** → **Vitals**

## For Caregivers

- iHealth devices are the simplest for seniors — one-button operation, large displays
- The blood pressure cuffs are especially senior-friendly
- Help with the one-time account setup (Step 2)
