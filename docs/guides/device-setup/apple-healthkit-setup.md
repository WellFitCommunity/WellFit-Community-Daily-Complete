# Apple Watch — Setup Guide

## Coming Soon

Apple Watch integration requires a companion iOS app that is currently in development.

## What Will Be Shared (when available)

| Data | How Often |
|------|-----------|
| Heart rate | Continuous |
| Blood oxygen (SpO2) | Daily |
| ECG | When recorded |
| Steps, distance, calories | Daily |
| Sleep duration and stages | Each night |
| Fall detection alerts | Real-time |

## In the Meantime

If you have an Apple Watch, you can still share your health data with your doctor through WellFit by:

1. **Manual check-ins** — Enter your readings during your daily WellFit check-in
2. **Bluetooth devices** — Use a Bluetooth blood pressure cuff, glucometer, or pulse oximeter (see [BLE Medical Devices guide](ble-medical-devices.md)) — these work on any device including iPhone
3. **Export from Apple Health** — Your doctor can request a health data export from the Apple Health app

## When Will Apple Watch Be Supported?

Apple HealthKit integration requires building a native iOS app. This is on our roadmap but does not have a release date yet. We'll notify you when it's available.
